Aptitude is how your mind connects information, evaluates options, and decides what to do next. Logic is the engine beneath every decision.

What Is Logic?

Logic is not intelligence and not education. It is the method your brain uses to move from information to action.

Logic = Perception + Rules + Memory + Evaluation

Two people can see the same situation and act completely differently because their logic engines are wired differently.

How Logic Is Formed in the Mind

Experience

The brain trusts experience more than advice. Repeated outcomes hardwire logical shortcuts.

Belief System

Beliefs act as hidden rules. Logic bends to protect what you already believe.

Emotional History

Past pain or reward biases logic toward avoidance or repetition.

Attention Training

What you repeatedly focus on becomes your default reasoning style.
