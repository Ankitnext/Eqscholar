Overview

Build routines that protect attention: anchors, scheduled deep work, and mini-rests.

Pomodoro + Rituals

Use 25/5 cycles and a pre-work ritual (breath or two). Reduce distractions: phone on Do Not Disturb.

Anchoring

Have a single visual cue to begin work (lamp on, specific playlist).

Mini-Checklist

  • Clear tasks
  • Timer set
  • Single window
  • Phone away
