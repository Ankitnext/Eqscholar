Overview

Tactics to convert inertia into small wins — environment, microtasks, and ritualized starts.

Micro-Task Method

Break big tasks into 5-minute actionable units. Start with the smallest step.

Ritualized Start

A simple pre-work ritual (tea, 3 breaths) signals your brain to begin.

Daily Checklist

  • List 3 microtasks
  • Pick one and do 5 minutes
  • Reward yourself with a tiny break
