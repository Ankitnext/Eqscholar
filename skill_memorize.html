Overview

Blend of ancient mnemonic methods and modern spaced repetition.

Technique 1 — Loci Method
Visualize a familiar place and 'place' items you want to remember.
Technique 2 — Chunking
Break information into smaller groups.
Technique 3 — Spaced Repetition
Review at increasing intervals.

Practice (5 min)

Try creating a memory palace for 5 grocery items.

Exercise

  1. Pick 5 words
  2. Create vivid images
  3. Place them in familiar spots
  4. Recall after 2 minutes

Quick Tips

Use vivid, emotional imagery; connect items to movement or smell for better recall.
